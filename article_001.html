GameJolt Post

Me (Jevilhumor) and Kasssm, are the two creators of an upcoming UNDERSWAP take. The take will follow a unique twist to UNDERTALEs story, and will also feature new interactions between you and the rest of the cast!

The game will be created in Gamemaker Studio 2, and will be shorter than UNDERTALE was. However, the game will keep the three ending types UNDERTALE had: Pacifist, No-Mercy, and Neutral.

The story already has a decent bit of progress, so it could take much less than one'd think!

Anyways, I think that's all I can reveal for now. Thanks for reading! Make sure you follow the GameJolt for future updates!
