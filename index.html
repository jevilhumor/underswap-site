Q&A
What's the difference between this and UNDERSWAP?
This take prioritizes delivering an UNDERTALE-tier story and tying the characters into said story in a natural and fulfilling way. Basically, just fixing the problems we had with the original UNDERSWAP.
What do you mean "improved swaps?"
"Improved swaps" referenced the fact that we kept the design philosophies, personalities, and other loveable attributes of the characters from UNDERTALE, and applied them to the UNDERSWAP formula.
What's the release plan?
After we divise an actual team and finish a rough draft of the entire story, we plan to flesh out and release a demo to give everyone a taste of the game so far.
How long do you think the game will be?
The game will be shorter than UNDERTALE, but will still feature Pacifist and No-Mercy endings amongst a few Neutral ones. If I had to estimate a time, the game would be around 4 ~ 5 hours in length for your first playthrough.
What happened to SPOOKYTALE?
SPOOKYTALE is being worked on. This project is just another one I wanted to make.
